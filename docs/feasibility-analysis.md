## Verdict: ✅ Feasible

- Ingredient cost is **~€5.57 per person** — very low relative to the €50/€60 price point
- **Gross margin on cooking: ~82-91%**
- **Gross margin on language: 100%** (no ingredients)
- Even at conservative 50% capacity, you clear **€1,566 profit** for the weekend
- At moderate 65%, **€2,286** — a solid weekend for two instructors

### Things that improve margin further:
- Buy pork in bulk from a talho (local butcher) — likely €4–5/kg instead of €6.50
- Korean ingredients from **Woori Mercado Coreano** (Rua Artilharia 1, Lisboa) or online — cheaper per kg in bulk
- Reusable cutting boards/knives/bowls — one-time cost ~€100 total for 14 sets

### Costs not included (to keep in mind):
- Instructor pay (your cost)
- Venue rental (if not your space)
- Marketing/advertising
- One-time equipment (cutting boards, knives, bowls, pots)
