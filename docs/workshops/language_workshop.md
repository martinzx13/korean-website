## Curriculum

### Session 1 — Hangul Foundation
- Korean alphabet (Hangul) — consonants and vowels
- Reading and writing simple syllables
- Basic greetings: 안녕하세요, 감사합니다, 네/아니요
- Introductions: 이름이 뭐예요?
- Cultural note: Korean writing systems history

### Session 2 — Everyday Conversations
- Numbers (Sino-Korean and Native Korean)
- Ordering food and asking for prices
- Basic verbs: to go, to eat, to drink
- Phrases for the cooking class: 맛있다, 더 주세요
- Role-play: at a restaurant

### Session 3 — Culture & Connection
- Review and conversation practice
- Family and relationships vocabulary
- Korean honorifics basics
- K-culture: common phrases from K-dramas and variety shows
- Q&A: ask anything about Korean language and culture

## Materials Provided
- Printed Hangul workbook
